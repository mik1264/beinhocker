# Power Law Extinction Distributions and Self-Organized Criticality

## Key Finding
Frequency distribution of extinctions of size s follows N(s) ~ s^(-α) with α ≈ 2.0-2.3, consistent with paleobiological evidence.

## Self-Organized Criticality in Evolution
- Systems move toward critical threshold triggering extinctions
- No external parameter tuning required
- Power law is primary evidence of SOC dynamics
- Both biotic and abiotic stresses contribute

## Implications for Simulation
- Our model should produce power-law distributed cascade sizes
- Log-log plot of cascade frequency vs size should show linear relationship
- Exponent α ≈ 2 is the target for realistic behavior
- Small cascades should be frequent, large cascades rare but present
