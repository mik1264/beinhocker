Key Insight

Harrington's devastating finding: the less frequent the transitions, the MORE dominant Rigids become, and the MORE catastrophic the eventual crash. Organizations that maintain a "reserve of Flexibles" — people who might seem less impressive in stable times — survive transitions with far less damage. This is the explore/exploit tradeoff in human form. The optimal organization sacrifices some efficiency during calm periods to buy insurance against disruption.
